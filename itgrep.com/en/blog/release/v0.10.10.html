Node v0.10.10 (Stable)

2013-06-05

2013.06.04, Version 0.10.10 (Stable)

  • uv: Upgrade to 0.10.10

  • npm: Upgrade to 1.2.25

  • url: Properly parse certain oddly formed urls (isaacs)

  • stream: unshift('') is a noop (isaacs)
