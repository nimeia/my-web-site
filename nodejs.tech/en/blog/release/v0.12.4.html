Node v0.12.4 (Stable)

2015-05-23

2015.05.22, Version 0.12.4 (Stable)

  • npm: upgrade to 2.10.1

  • V8: revert v8 Array.prototype.values() removal (cjihrig)

  • win: bring back xp/2k3 support (Bert Belder)
